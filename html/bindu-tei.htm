This is the secret teaching of the scriptures of Yoga in all of the scriptures.The YSv introduces a section with ``idaṃ yogaśāstrarahasyañ \ldots '' but Rāmacandra's version of the yogaśāstrarahasyam differs drastically. Just as the light of a lamp enters into the midst of darkness, similarly, his mind enters into the teaching.
