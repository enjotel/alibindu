In the Yogatattvabindu, the twelve kalās of the sun represent the various qualities and aspects of the sun's influence. Perhaps the number twelve additionally reflects the twelve signs of the zodiac or the twelve months in a year. The ten kalās of the fire in the Yogatattvabindu represent the various qualities and aspects of the fire's influence. Now, the twelve digits of the sun are taught. 1. Tāpinī, 2. Grāsikā, 3. Ugrā, 4.
